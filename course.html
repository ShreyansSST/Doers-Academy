About Course

The sweet and healthy continuation of running successful businesses like Breakstation, Comedy Circle, The Doers and getting to understand the mindset of more than 140+ entrepreneurs, we formed and acquired plenty of information and documented them into a full length course worth of 25 hours. The Masterclass is designed for people who are trying to step forward in the world of digital marketing and content creation.

Who is this Course For ?

  • STUDENTS
  • ENTREPRENEURS
  • INTRAPRENEURS
  • BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT OFFICER
  • DIGITAL MARKETING ENTHUSIASTS/ DIGITAL MARKETER

What will I Learn?

  • IDENTIFICATION:
    How to identify content types and forms for any businesses and services to increase its demand and acquire more customers
  • PRODUCTION:
    How to identify which form of content is more relevant and contextual? How to approach it? Learning Pre-Production, Production & Post-Production.
  • DISTRIBUTION:
    Identifying the platforms where the probable audience is based. Identifying the form where your audience consumes the content and finding the right way of distributing your product to these audiences.
  • ACQUISITION:
    How to build a funnel to acquire a customer using your content?
